KARATE

Karate is a popular martial art that originated in Okinawa, Japan. It is both an effective form of self-defense and a great way to stay fit while learning important life skills and values. Karate training is divided into three main parts:
Kihon (basics): Includes fundamental techniques like strikes, blocks, kicks, and stances. Practicing these helps students refine their movements and use their bodies efficiently.
Kata (forms): Pre-arranged sequences of movements that simulate combat. They help develop coordination, balance, agility, strength, and speed.
Kumite (sparring): The application of Kihon and Kata techniques against an opponent. For beginners, sparring is pre-arranged and practiced in a slow, controlled way.
Overall, Karate improves both physical ability and mental discipline. More
